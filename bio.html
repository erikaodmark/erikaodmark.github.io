My Biography

Erika Odmark grew up in Seattle, Washington and graduated from Lakeside School as a National AP Scholar with Honors. She is a double major in Mathematics and Computer Science at Tufts University, where she plans to graduate in December '18. Erika is on the Tufts rowing team, and has been competing for the past eight years. During summer '17, she was an intern at Microsoft, where she worked with a group of peers to design, user test, and implement an app for students and teachers. In her free time, she enjoys painting, hiking and knitting.
